What Are Stars?

  1. Stars are massive celestial bodies made mostly of hydrogen and helium that produce light and heat.
  2. They are the building blocks of galaxies and there are basically infinite stars in the universe.

Interesting Facts About Stars

  • Our sun is a star.
  • Stars are different temperatures based on their color.
  • When some stars die, they end in a supernova.
